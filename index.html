About

Hello! My name is Alodivinno Ricco Cantos. I am a first-year Computer Science student studying at Batangas State University – The National Engineering University. With a love for technology and design, I have dedicated my time to creating user-centered, high-quality digital experiences that solve problems and delight users.

I began my journey in June of last year, and I have developed a strong foundation in HTML and CSS. Currently, we are learning C++. My passion for coding and continuous learning has allowed me to keep up with the latest industry trends and best practices. I have worked on a variety of projects, ranging from an HTML registration form to a fully functioning portfolio. One of my proudest achievements was developing a program in C++ that uses CRUD operations to create a planner. It was an experimental solution, and I delivered it as part of my learning process. My approach is centered around collaboration, creativity, and user-first design. I believe in building solutions that not only look great but also function seamlessly across all platforms. I am always looking for ways to push boundaries and innovate. Looking ahead, I’m excited to continue expanding my skill set and am always seeking new challenges where I can grow both professionally and personally.
